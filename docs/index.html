ABOUT

My name is Kai Hashimoto and above all, my joy in life is learning. From CAD to SQL, CFD to metal fabrication, I find fufillment in applying what I know to build cool things. Between 2024-2025 I was fortunate to be the Team Manager for Blue Sky Solar Racing, a 50-student design team where we built our lightest car yet, the Aurora - Leading the construction of the aero-shell, all the way to orchestrating the logistics of competing in the 2025 Formula Sun Grand Prix.

Currently, I'm an advisor / board member for the Blue Sky team. My long-term goal is to create an Enterprise Resource Planner and building a Quadcopter for fun. You can find out more about these projects and what I do, under the Projects page, as this website serves as both as a blog and portfolio.

As a challenge to myself, this website was made from scratch by me using HTML/CSS/JS without the assistance of AI tools.
